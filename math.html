Math Department

Mission Statement


The Wilkinson High School Math department is dedicated toward the equal education of all students, and bestowing upon them quantitative and critical thinking skills. After completing the required Mathematics courses during their time here at Wilkinson, students will be able to provide more as prospecive members of the modern workforce.
