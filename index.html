New Gymnasium Unveiled!


Renovations have started on a new gymnasium, and the overall construction will be completed over the summer by the start of the next school year. The gymnasium will get brand-new seating, a repolished floor, and a lighting system. This is the 3rd athletics-related renovation within the past decade for WHS, and soon more may come! Read More
