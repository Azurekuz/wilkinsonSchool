Athletics

Go Wilkinson Walruses!


Wilkinson High School provides its students a plethora of athletic activities and extracurriculars to jump into and participate in. We encourage students to be fit in body, as well as their minds. Student athletes are held accountable for maintaining their GPA above a 2.5, and their grades above a C-.


IMPORTANT NEWS

Win for Football Team!

Another win for the walrus, given to us by Wilkinson's Football team in their game against the Glendale Groundhogs last weekend with a final score of 24-18!

New Gymnasium!

Renovations have started on a new gymnasium, and the overall construction will be completed over the summer by the start of the next school year. The gymnasium will get brand-new seating, a repolished floor, and a lighting system. This is the 3rd athletics-related renovation within the past decade for WHS, and soon more may come!

Scholarships Available

Scholarship applications are now open for student athletes to apply for. Scholarship applications are due by May 7th, and results will be announced on May 25th during Senior night! Available scholarships include (but not limited to): Jackson A. Lowe Scholarship, Isaias R. Floyd Athletic Scholar Fund, John P. Walker Athletic Grant, George M. Barns Award, and the Arthur H. Lee Memorial Scholarship.

Upcoming Women's Tennis Game

This Saturday, April 28th, Wilkinson's Girls Varsity Tennis team are facing the Ridgeview Ragdolls. Come cheer them on at our tennis courts at 10AM, this Saturday! Tickets are $10 for adults, $7 for kids, $5 for students, and free for student athletes.

Loss for Baseball Team

The WHS Boys Varsity Baseball team suffered a humiliating loss last weekend against the Chattanooga Sheeps, from Chattenooga High School. The final score was a crushing 8-21. This has been WHS's 4th loss in a row, can the team even hope to recover, or should they simply lay down their bats, and walk away? We shall see how this ends with the final set of games this month.
